Who are we?

Everyday People is a nonprofit organization founded and operated by a group of high school students. All 40 of our volunteers have a passion for helping people, and making our state the best that it can be.

Why do we exist?

Everyday People was created in the aftermath of the Minneapolis protests. We have seen many citizens struggling to get by, family-owned businesses destroyed, and little hope left in our city. We want to change that.

What are we doing?

There are a few projects we are currently working on. Firstly, we are working on getting the essentials, food/water/supplies, to Minneapolis citizens. We are also helping to remove the graffiti and vandalism that occured during the protests.

Help us today.

We would love to see you volunteering! We need as much help as possible in order to make Minneapolis a better place. Thank you.
